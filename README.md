## 📖 About YRAN
Young Resilient Advocacy Network (YRAN) is a youth-led organization based in Mathare, Nairobi. We are committed to empowering young people, especially adolescent girls and young women, to become leaders and change-makers in their communities.

---

## 🎯 Thematic Areas
YRAN focuses on four key areas:

- 💼 Economic Empowerment  
- ❤️ Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR)  
- 🌱 Climate Change  
- 🗳️ Social Accountability  

---

## 🚀 Our Mission
To empower young people with knowledge, skills, and opportunities to create sustainable and impactful change in their communities.
